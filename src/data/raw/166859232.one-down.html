Welp, time to get a new book. This one’s full.

I started this little project after reading about Austin Kleon’s logbook. The earliest pages are pretty clinical with a time indexed log of mundane notes like when I woke up, how many naps I took, and my mood throughout the day. But that sort of things has never been very interesting to look back on. It took about a month of experimentation for the pages to start to resemble their current form. Makes me wonder what they’ll look like in another 6 months.
