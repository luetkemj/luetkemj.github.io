Smokey died in March of 2011 quite unexpectedly. The vet said he was between 18 and 22 years old. From what we know of his history he had been a neighborhood tom for many years before a friend of ours took him in. Eventually our friend’s work began to require more and more travel and Smokey moved in with us.

Smokey was one of those one in a million cats. He looked like a crank but was really just one of the sweetest old cats you could ever hope to meet. He was kind with his stuffies, particular in all the best worst ways, and wore that tuxedo like he was born with it.

Without fail, he would always make an annoyance of himself by jumping up on the table and curling up right in the middle of my workspace. Strange, the things you miss.
