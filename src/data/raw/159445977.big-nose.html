Big Nose is our favorite deer at Horsetooth. She’s two-ish, I think. As a fawn she had a disproportionately large nose. It was adorable. She also had more than her share of curiosity. No fear of humans. Which made us plenty afraid for her.

This fall there was a pair of new fawns born much too late in the season. I worried for them too. Would they make it through a harsh winter? Was Big Nose a momma? An aunt? Would she join the deer squad looking out for the wobbly kneed?

There’s more than just cold weather and cars to worry about.

Lions gotta eat too.
