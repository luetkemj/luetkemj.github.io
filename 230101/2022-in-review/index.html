2022 In Review

I keep track of my runs in Strava and in a spreadsheet. Every run brings back a little memory. A snapshot from the trail, a smell, or a feeling. And each run, as I dredge it up from the memory banks, pulls with it the surrounding events in my life. So here's 2022 as punctuated by runs - and a whole lot of other shit.

January

  • a cold 20 miles in St. Ed's
  • Iris is acquired by a fully remote company
  • fully commit to moving to CO
  • blown hamstring racing Remus in my first injury since highschool
  • begin to prep house for sale

February

  • fly to CO to look at homes in the Denver area
  • Remus turns 16

March

  • Jen flies back to CO to meet with the realtor and look at homes
  • start to slowly run again after injury
  • put a failed offer on a home in Littleton
  • take another look at our original first choice city of Fort Collins

April

  • put a successful offer on a home in Fort Collins

May

  • Jen turns 40
  • put our house on the market in Austin
  • our house in Austin sells
  • fly to CO to sign closing paperwork
  • first official summit of Horsetooth
  • close remotely on house in Austin
  • last group run with the Austin Dirt Runners
  • kids last day of school in Austin
  • finish packing up the house and load the moving truck
  • roadtrip from Austin to Fort Collins

June

  • first group run with Gnar - definitely out of shape and feeling the altitude
  • first towers - we're not in hill country anymore...
  • realize I need to start putting in the effort and get consistant in order to enjoy the outdoors here
  • think about getting serious with my runs but don't

July

  • make a trail friend out on Coyote Ridge
  • trip to Atlanta to see family, ending the longest continuous stretch of time I'd ever been in CO
  • Jen goes back to work

August

  • drive to Montrose to pick up the truck topper
  • first Arthur's with Jen
  • second Arthur's with Kate
  • Rocky Mountain National Park with Kate before she moves to Africa
  • kids start new schools in Fort Collins
  • run into the defending and soon to be two time leadville 100 champ on Coyote Ridge
  • first Lory run
  • after dark Horsetooth by myself
  • another failed attempt with Remus to bag our first fourteener - no dice on Democrat

September

  • Red Feather Jamboree half marathon - first Colorado trail race
  • make an actual running plan to build a base in prep for the Gnar mini slam in '23

October

  • darkmode
  • Horsetooth group run wherein I meet some local legends and finally start to connect the dots
  • a few days in Estes with the Phillips fam
  • whirlwind work trip in DC
  • Halloween

November

  • consistent training begins to pay off and I have my first successful group run with the fast folks
  • my first and second As
  • Frankie turns 7
  • Thanksgiving
  • start running with my neighbor for the midweek miles

December

  • turn 40
  • the creative engine finally begins to spark to life
  • snag the sunrise/sunset winter solstice badge
  • coldest run of my life at -17f
  • 20th wedding anniversary
  • Christmas eve
  • enjoying new fitness with a 10 miler at 7:49 per mile on Christmas day
  • 14.2 miles on the local hike & bikes to cap off the year
